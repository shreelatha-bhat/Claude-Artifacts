## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP — Current Priority (FY2024–2025)

| Dimension | Criteria |
|-----------|---------|
| **Geography** | Bangalore city or surrounding metro (Whitefield, Electronic City, Outer Ring Road corridor) |
| **Industry** | Manufacturing (automotive, aerospace, chemicals, FMCG, heavy engineering) OR Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs, government enterprises) |
| **Company Size** | 500–5,000 employees |
| **Tech Situation** | Running SAP ECC or Oracle EBS — NOT yet migrated to S/4HANA or cloud ERP |
| **Pain Trigger** | 2027 SAP ECC mainstream maintenance deadline — actively evaluating options |
| **Budget Signal** | CIO-level initiative, digital transformation budget allocated or under approval |

**Why this ICP:** This segment has forced urgency (the 2027 deadline is non-negotiable),
meaningful budget, and a problem 7EDGE is specifically equipped to solve. The Bangalore
geography reduces sales cycle friction via in-person engagement.

### Secondary ICP — Ongoing (Global)

- SaaS companies (USA/UK/UAE/Singapore) looking to scale their AWS infrastructure
- Mid-size enterprises with custom software modernization needs
- Technology companies building new product lines on AWS-native stack

## Buying Signals

### Strong Signals (prioritize these accounts)

- **SAP ECC or Oracle EBS** mentioned in job postings, LinkedIn company profile, or press
- **CIO / Head of IT / VP Digital Transformation** hiring activity — signals active investment
- **"SAP S/4HANA migration"**, **"cloud strategy"**, **"ERP modernization"** in recent news,
  press releases, or leadership LinkedIn posts
- **AWS partner program** or AWS-related procurement activity
- **IT infrastructure refresh** announcements or tenders
- Recent **funding rounds or government allocation** for digital modernization (especially PSUs)

### Moderate Signals (worth researching)

- Manufacturing company with 1,000+ employees in Bangalore region
- PSU with "digital India" or "Industry 4.0" mandate
- Technology-related hiring spike at a traditionally non-tech company
- Legacy stack (Java EE, .NET Framework, on-prem Oracle DB) in job postings

### Disqualifying Signals (deprioritize)

- Already live on SAP S/4HANA or cloud ERP (opportunity passed)
- Startup or company under 200 employees (not 7EDGE's scale)
- Pure services company with no enterprise system complexity
- Active Big 4 SI engagement locked in for ERP migration

## Target Personas

### Primary Decision Makers

**CIO / CITO**
- Cares about: strategic risk reduction, cost control, board-level compliance, vendor accountability
- Pain: Being personally responsible if SAP ECC is still running when support ends in 2027
- Message: De-risk the transition with a proven partner. 500+ delivered projects. AWS-certified.

**VP / Head of IT**
- Cares about: technical feasibility, team capability, delivery risk, not embarrassing themselves
- Pain: Doesn't know how to evaluate the migration path. Big SIs feel too expensive and slow.
- Message: 7EDGE will do the assessment first — no commitment required upfront. Here's what the path looks like.

**Head of Digital Transformation**
- Cares about: strategic roadmap, vendor alignment with transformation goals, innovation narrative
- Pain: Has a mandate but no trusted technical implementation partner
- Message: We don't just migrate — we modernize. The outcome is a system that supports your transformation agenda.

### Secondary Influencers

**CFO**
- Cares about: SAP extended maintenance cost vs. migration cost, ROI timeline, budget certainty
- Message: Extended SAP maintenance post-2027 will cost X. Migration now is a one-time investment with long-term savings.

**Plant IT Head (Manufacturing)**
- Cares about: operational continuity, no downtime, integration with plant systems (MES, SCADA)
- Message: 7EDGE has done phased migrations for manufacturing environments. We design around operational constraints.

## Sales Motion & Stages

### Typical Deal Flow

```
Lead Qualification
    ↓
Discovery Call (ICP fit, ERP situation, timeline, budget signal)
    ↓
Technical Discovery (system landscape, integration complexity, team capability)
    ↓
Assessment / Proof of Value (optional paid engagement: cloud readiness or migration roadmap)
    ↓
Roadmap Presentation (what the migration path looks like, phased approach, estimated scope)
    ↓
Commercial Proposal (phased engagement, milestone-based pricing)
    ↓
Negotiation & SOW
    ↓
Kickoff
```

### Key Stage Notes

- **Assessment engagement** is a major trust-builder and revenue-de-risker. When the
  prospect is uncertain, propose a paid 4–6 week assessment before the full engagement.
  This converts hesitation into commitment and surfaces scope risks early.

- **Roadmap presentation** is the highest-value sales moment. A concrete, credible
  migration roadmap — built on real discovery — closes more deals than any proposal deck.

- **Avoid big-bang proposals.** Phased scoping (Phase 1: Assessment + Architecture;
  Phase 2: Core Migration; Phase 3: Optimization) reduces commitment anxiety and gets
  deals started faster.

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|---------|
| "We're already talking to Wipro / Infosys / TCS" | "Big SIs are good at managing large programs. 7EDGE is good at building and delivering complex software. If you want accountability and engineering depth, not account management, we're worth a conversation." |
| "You're too small" | "Our size means you get senior engineers, not fresh graduates. 500+ delivered projects means we've solved your problem before. Size is the wrong metric — track record is the right one." |
| "We're not sure about the 2027 timeline" | "SAP has been consistent. Extended maintenance exists but is expensive — and the longer you wait, the less time you have to migrate without disruption. The right time to start planning is now, even if execution starts later." |
| "Can you handle our scale?" | "Tell us what 'scale' means to you — team size, data volume, transaction load. We'll be honest if it's outside our sweet spot. But 500+ projects across USA, UK, UAE suggests we've handled complexity before." |
| "We need a local partner" | "We're Bangalore-based. In-person engagement is our default, not a premium add-on." |
| "Your rates are higher than other vendors" | "Compare what you're buying. 7EDGE assigns senior engineers. Many vendors quote low and staff junior. The total cost of a failed or delayed migration is far higher than the rate difference." |
