## Delivery Model

7EDGE's delivery model is a deliberate differentiator from large SIs and from pure
resource-augmentation firms:

**Senior-led engagement model:**
- Principal/senior engineers are assigned, not junior resources managed remotely
- Engagements are outcome-oriented, not time-and-material body shops
- Direct access to engineering leadership throughout the engagement

**Typical engagement lifecycle:**
1. **Discovery & Assessment** — Understand the system landscape, constraints, goals
2. **Roadmap & Architecture** — Define the modernization or migration path
3. **Phased Delivery** — Iterative, milestone-driven execution with clear scope per phase
4. **Stabilization & Handoff** — Ensure client team can own and operate the outcome

**Engagement types:**
- Fixed-scope projects (most common for migration work)
- Time-bound modernization programs (multi-phase, outcome-oriented)
- Platform engineering retainers (DevOps, infrastructure, observability)
- Advisory and assessment engagements (cloud readiness, architecture reviews)

## Culture & Engineering Philosophy

Use these talking points in employer brand content, job descriptions, and culture-focused
communications:

- **Engineering depth over headcount growth.** 7EDGE scales by deepening capability, not
  by adding bodies. Every engineer is expected to be a practitioner, not a coordinator.

- **Ownership culture.** Engineers own their modules end-to-end — architecture,
  implementation, deployment, and post-go-live. No handoffs as an excuse.

- **Product thinking in services.** 7EDGE thinks like a product company even when
  delivering services — this means prioritizing long-term system health over
  short-term delivery metrics.

- **AWS-first, cloud-native default.** Every architectural decision starts with "how would
  this work natively on AWS?" — not with lifting a pattern from on-premise thinking.

- **Transparent, principal-level engagement.** Clients speak to the engineers building
  their systems. No account manager layer obscuring the technical reality.
